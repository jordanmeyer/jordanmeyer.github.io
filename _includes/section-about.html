Jordan Meyer is a machine learning scientist and winner of the Zillow Prize. His art applies cutting edge artificial intelligence techniques to create large format (4k+) works of inimitable richness and depth.

"I take a mentor's approach to building neural networks for generative art. By slowly exposing the models to curated imagery and encouraging their experimentation, their creative worldview is formed like a student's would be. While most AI-generative models are single-purpose, and by that I mean they are trained on a static set of images and then used to generate similar ones, I continue to train the same models on fundamentals, and allow their artistic understandings to mature over time."
